What's your take on DBC/EDA experience?

I think the DBC/EDA experience is very engaging and focused. I think that it is great that it teaches programming, and empithy but also exposes us to new environments that we will be working in.

What is your impressions?

My impression of EDA is I don't think that it will be easy, it seems to be a lot of work in a small amount of time but I do think it will be a great experience.

How do you see yourself engaging with this culture?

I think I will take a bit to warm up to this way of learning and working. I am use to working in a team in hospitality, but I do think this will be different.

Have your expectations changed?

My expectations have not changed, I did quite a bit of research and reading of blogs before applying so I think my expectations were fairly correct.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

I am very excited, extremely nervous because I think it will be very different to what I am use to, but I am looking forward to it.
